# Billing Management


* * *

## How to manage your billing?

  * Navigate to the My account dropdown and click on **Add to credit balance** or **Buy credit to start using API** to access your billing information and settings ![file](https://fptcloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/1-1.png)
  * You can view the pricing for **AI Marketplace - Model as a service** ![file](https://fptcloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/2.png)
  * To buy credit, click **Purchase** button. You can choose to top up with the recommended amount, such as **1,500,000 VND** or **2,500,000 VND** , or enter your desired amount. The minimum transaction is **26,000 VND,** then click "**Purchase** ". ![file](https://fptcloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/3.png)
  * You will be redirected to the **VNPay** payment to complete the transaction with your selected amount using your preferred payment methods: **VNPayQR** or **International payment cards** ,...


![file](https://fptcloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/image-1742892528929.png)

  * After a top-up, check **Credit History** to view your top-up transaction history. ![file](https://fptcloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/image-1742892550518.png)
