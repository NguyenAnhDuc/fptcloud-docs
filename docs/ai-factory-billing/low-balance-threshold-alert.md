# Low Balance Threshold Alert


* * *

Running out of credits may disrupt your applications on **FPT AI Factory**. To avoid this, you can enable **Low Balance Alerts**. These alerts notify you by email when your balance drops below a threshold you define.  
By default, this feature is **disabled**.

![Alt text](https://fptcloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/image2025-9-16_14-55-51.png)

### How to Enable Low Balance Alerts


  3. Set your **Credit Threshold** – the balance amount at which you want to be notified. 


### Recommendation

For uninterrupted service, we recommend setting your threshold to receive at least **three alert emails** about your balance, giving you enough time to recharge credits before they run out.
