# Delete SSL Certificate


* * *

To delete an **SSL Certificate** , follow the steps below:

**Step 1:** On the **SSL Certificate** screen, click the trash bin icon to delete the **SSL Certificate.**

**Step 2:** A confirmation dialog will appear. Click **Delete SSL Certificate** to proceed with the deletion, or **Cancel** to abort.
