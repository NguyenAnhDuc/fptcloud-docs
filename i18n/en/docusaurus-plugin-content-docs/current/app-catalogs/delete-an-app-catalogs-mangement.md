# Delete an App Catalogs Mangement


* * *

The system allows deleting an App Catalogs Management in case users stopped using the product. To do so, follow these steps:

**Step 1** : From the **App Catalogs Management** screen, hover on the **Actions** button of the desired Catalog and click **Delete**.

![](https://fptcloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/Picture38-1.png)

**Step 2** : In the confirmation popup, type **delete** to confirm and click **Confirm** to execute deletion of the App Catalog.

![](https://fptcloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/Picture39-1.png)
