# Payment Model


* * *

## PAYG

Pay-as-you-go (PAYG) is a flexible payment model where you only pay for the resources you use, with no upfront commitment. Charges are calculated based on actual usage, such as compute time or storage. 

For example, if you rent a container with one H100 GPU (15 CPUs, 250 GB RAM, 1 GPU) at a price of $2.31 per hour, running your container for 15 minutes will cost $2.31 × 0.25 = $0.5775. We will deduct this amount directly from your balance as soon as the calculation is complete every 15 minutes. You can get a detailed billing history by clicking "Credit History" within the Billing page of the console.
