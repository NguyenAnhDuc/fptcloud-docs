# Add Credits To Account


* * *

If your balance is insufficient to create a new GPU container, please top up your account and try again by following these steps:

  2. You have to **choose a currency** if it is the first time you access Billing. ![Alt text](https://fptcloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/image2025-6-22_11-39-26.png)
  3. Click the **Add credit** button ![Alt text](https://fptcloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/image2025-6-22_11-49-23.png)
  4. In the **Add credit pop-up** , you can select a suggested amount or **enter the amount of credits** you want to add to your balance. ![Alt text](https://fptcloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/image2025-6-22_11-47-16.png)

     * **VNPay** : Only supports the Vietnam region and Vietnamese dongs. You will complete the transaction on the **VNPay** checkout website.
     * **Stripe** : Enter the card details in the pop-up window to proceed with the payment. **All transactions are securely processed by Stripe, and FPT does not store card information.**

![Alt text](https://fptcloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/image2025-6-22_11-55-30.png)

  6. After the payment is completed, a pop-up will appear displaying a success notification, and your balance will be updated accordingly. ![Alt text](https://fptcloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/Picture1.png)
