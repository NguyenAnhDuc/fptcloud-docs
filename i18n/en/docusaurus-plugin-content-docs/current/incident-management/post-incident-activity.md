# Post Incident Activity


* * *

You can add brief notes during the incident handling process. Doing so would help you in term of incident management activities, prevention or mitigation of similar future incidents.

### A. Create an Activity

**Step 1** : From the sidebar menu, choose **Apps** , then click **FMON - Incident**

**Step 2** : Click **Home** to view a list of incidents

**Step 3** : Click on the title of the incident to view its detail

**Step 4** : Provide your notes in the text box.

**Step 5** : Click **Add note**.

![](https://fptcloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/11/Screenshot_18.png)

### B. Update an Activity

**Step 1** : Click on the title of the incident to view its detail

**Step 2** : Hover to the activity that you want to edit, then click on the three dot icon in the upper right corner and click the **Edit Note** option.

![](https://fptcloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/11/Screenshot_19.png)

**Step 3** : Update the Activity information

**Step 4** : Click **Save**.

### C. Delete an Activity

**Step 1** : Click on the title of the incident to view its detail

**Step 2** : Hover to the activity that you want to delete, then click on the three dot icon in the upper right corner and click the **Delete Activity** option.

![](https://fptcloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/11/Screenshot_19.png)

### D. Commenting on an Activity

**Step 1** : Click on the title of the incident to view its detail

**Step 2** : Hover to the activity you want to add a comment, then click on the **Comment** icon in the upper right corner

![](https://fptcloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/11/Screenshot_20.png)

**Step 3** : Enter your text into the textbox.

**Step 4** : Click **Save**.
