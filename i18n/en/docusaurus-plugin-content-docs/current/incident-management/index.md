# Incident Management


Incident Management


:::info
🇻🇳 **Phiên bản tiếng Việt:** [Đọc bằng tiếng Việt](/incident-management)
:::


FPT Smart Cloud officially launched the **Incident Management** feature. **Incident Management** is an intelligent incident management tool designed to help you quickly respond to disruptive and unexpected events. 

With the ability to integrate with Jira and Webhook tools. **Incident Management** simplifies your incident response process, reducing the time and effort required to manage incidents.
