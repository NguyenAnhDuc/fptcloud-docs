# Incident Status


* * *

The **status of the incident** indicates whether it has been resolved or not. The incident status must immediately show whether the incident is still being mitigated.

Below is a list of recommended statuses that are given in the FMON incident system:

**Status** | **Description**  
---|---  
Active | Incident occurred  
Resolved | Incident has been resolved  
  
### A. Editing incident status

**Step 1** : From the sidebar menu, choose **Apps** , then click **FMON - Incident**

**Step 2** : Click **Settings**

**Step 3** : In the **Status** tab, choose an existing incident status, then click the **Edit** icon.

![](https://fptcloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/11/Screenshot_13.png)

**Step 4** : Edit the **Name** and **Description** for the label if desired.

**Step 5** : Click **Edit Status** to update the incident status

![](https://fptcloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/11/Screenshot_14.png)
