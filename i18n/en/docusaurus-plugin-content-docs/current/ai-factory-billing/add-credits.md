# How to add credits?


* * *

To start using the AI Factory services with the PAYG billing plan, you need to buy credits first.

Follow the instructions below:


  2. Click the **Add credit** button ![Alt text](https://fptcloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/image2025-6-22_11-49-23.png)

  3. In the **Add Credit pop-up** , you can select a suggested amount or **enter the amount of credits you** want to add to your balance. *_A minimum amount is required—you can't add less._ ![Alt text](https://fptcloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/image2025-7-6_17-32-55.png)


     * **VNPay** : Only supports the Vietnam region and Vietnamese dongs. You will complete the transaction on the VNPay checkout website.
     * **PayPal** : Only supports the Vietnam region and US dollar. You will complete the transaction on the PayPal checkout pop-up.
     * **Stripe** : Enter the card details in the pop-up window to proceed with the payment. **All transactions are securely processed by Stripe, and FPT does not store card information.**


An OTP may be required to verify the payment transaction. It depends on your card issuer. ![Alt text](https://fptcloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/image2025-7-6_17-34-54.png) ![Alt text](https://fptcloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/08/image2025-8-10_11-23-14.png)

  5. After the payment is completed, a pop-up will appear displaying a success notification, and your **Total Balance** and **Top-up Balance** will be updated accordingly. ![Alt text](https://fptcloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/Picture1-1.png)

  6. You can also check out your adding credit transaction in the **Credit history** table. ![Alt text](https://fptcloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/image2025-6-28_12-17-42.png)
