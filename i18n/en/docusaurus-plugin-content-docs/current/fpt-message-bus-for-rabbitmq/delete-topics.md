# Delete Topics


* * *

**Step 1** : From the **Application** menu > **Topics** > Choose a desired topic > **Remove**.

![](https://fptcloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/11/topic_delete_1.png)

**Step 2** : A Confirmation dialog will appear. Click **Yes** to delete the topic or **No** to cancel the operation.

![](https://fptcloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/11/topic_delete_2.png)

_Note: When you delete a topic, all subscriptions registered within that topic will also be removed._
