# Completion History


* * *

Select a session and open the **Completion History** tab to view all past completions for that session. ![file](https://fptcloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/image-1746697738348.39.04.png)

  * You can choose your preferred completion from the list or download the full history of text completions.

  * Click Continue to test the model using your selected completion.
